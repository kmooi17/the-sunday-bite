A little about me and why I love baking:

I love baking because it's a core passion of mine. I enjoy people's reactions when they bite into my treats. It always puts a massive smile of my face. My dream is to have my own bakery one day so I can put smiles on all of my customer's faces. My specialties include the gourmet cookies, however I do also make lamingtons, red cakes, snowballs and cake pops on request.
